Prologue

John Tuomey is a renowned Irish architect, educator, and writer. Tuomey studied architecture at the University College Dublin (UCD) and emerged as a leading voice in the discipline, emphasizing the importance of context, materiality, and craftsmanship in his work. He founded O’Donnel +Tuomey in 1988 with his spouse, Sheila O’Donnel. Tuomey is also a respected academic, having taught at the UCD and other leading institutions such as Harvard University’s Graduate School of Design and the Architectural Association in London. His teachings focus on the interplay between the architecture and its environment, emphasising the importance of storytelling and historical continuity in design.
